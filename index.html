📅 Lok Sabha Election Timeline

Announcement

ECI announces election schedule; Model Code of Conduct activates immediately

Nomination Filing

Candidates file nominations at Returning Officer's office (7–14 days window)

Scrutiny & Withdrawal

Nominations scrutinized; candidates may withdraw within 2 days

Campaign Period

Active campaigning; silent period 48 hrs before polling

Polling Day

Voting via EVM + VVPAT; 7am–6pm typically

Counting & Results

Votes counted; winner declared by Returning Officer

📖 Key Terms

ECI
Election Commission of India — autonomous body that oversees all elections
EVM
Electronic Voting Machine — used since 1982, tamper-proof voting device
VVPAT
Voter Verified Paper Audit Trail — prints a slip so voter can verify their choice
MCC
Model Code of Conduct — guidelines parties must follow once election is announced
Constituency
A geographic area that elects one representative to the Lok Sabha
NOTA
None of the Above — option added in 2013; allows rejecting all candidates
